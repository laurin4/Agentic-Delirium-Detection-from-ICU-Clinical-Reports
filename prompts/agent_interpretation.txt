Your task:

- Output stays within signal strength only (niedrig / mittel / hoch). Do NOT output multiclass 0/1/2.

- **Binary clinical question (for downstream mapping):** Is delirium the best explanation for the documented findings?

- **niedrig** → delirium unlikely or excluded.

-------------------------------------
Treat as strong support for **hoch** (unless clearly negated in snippets):

-------------------------------------
You may assign **mittel** or **hoch** when clinically coherent combinations appear, for example:

Do NOT assign **mittel** or **hoch** based only on a single weak finding such as:

5. PROPHYLAXIS / CONDITIONAL REFERENCES (never positive alone)

Do NOT classify as delirium based only on:
- Delirprophylaxe, Delirscreening, Delirrisiko
